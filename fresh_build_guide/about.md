# About the Project (GBR - Ghana Bus Route)

## Overview

GBR (Ghana Bus Route) is a comprehensive bus ticketing, booking, and fleet management web application. It is designed to streamline the operations of a modern transport company by digitizing the process of ticket sales, fleet scheduling, and passenger management.

## Key Features & Capabilities

### 1. Online Ticket Booking

- **User Portal**: Passengers can browse available routes, view bus schedules, and book tickets online.
- **Mobile Money Integration**: Integration with major Mobile Money (MoMo) providers in Ghana (MTN, Telecel, AT) for seamless and secure payment processing.
- **Ticket Management**: Automated ticket generation with issue and expiry dates, allowing passengers to view and manage their valid tickets.

### 2. Fleet & Trip Management

- **Bus Registration**: Tracking of the company fleet including details like registration numbers, capacity, make, model, year, and operational condition (e.g., operational, maintenance, damaged).
- **Driver Allocation**: Managing driver profiles and licenses, allowing assignment to specific trips.
- **Trip Scheduling**: Creating and managing trips that link a specific bus, driver, and route, calculating departure times and estimated times of arrival (ETA).

### 3. Route & Terminal Management

- **Regional Setup**: Classification of the country by regions, cities, and specific city codes mapping directly to operational areas.
- **Terminals**: Establishing boarding and dropping points across various regions.
- **Route Pricing**: Defining start terminals and end terminals with dynamically assigned pricing for each trip.

### 4. Admin and Station Agent Systems

- **Role-Based Access Control**: Differentiating access between System Administrators, Station Agents, and standard Users.
- **Agent Booking**: Allowing agents positioned at terminals to manually issue tickets for walk-in customers.
- **Special Services (Bus Hiring)**: A module dedicated to processing excursion or private company bus hiring requests from the public, managing approvals and statuses.

## Target Audience

- **The Public**: Daily commuters or travelers looking for a reliable way to book long-distance or intercity bus trips entirely online.
- **Internal Staff**: Transport companies aiming to digitalize operations and track everything from ticket sales to vehicle maintenance centrally.
